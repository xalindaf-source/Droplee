Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information Droplee collects when you use our site, and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account details: your name and email address when you sign up
  • Order details: what you order, from which store, your delivery address, and order totals
  • Payment information: processed securely by Paystack — Droplee does not store your card details

How we use it

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To notify stores of new orders so they can prepare them
  • To communicate with you about your order status

Who we share it with

The store you order from receives your order details and delivery address so they can fulfil your order. Payment processing is handled by Paystack under their own privacy and security standards. We don't sell your information to third parties.

Your data

You can request to see or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us — see our support page for contact details.
