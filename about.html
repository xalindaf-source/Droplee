About Droplee

Droplee started with a simple idea: if a local store sells it, you should be able to get it delivered — fast, without hassle, without needing a dozen different apps for a dozen different shops.

We connect customers with local businesses across the Garden Route, starting right here in Mossel Bay, Voorbaai, Danabaai and Hartenbos. Whether it's your favourite health store, a pharmacy run, or something from a shop you didn't even know delivered — Droplee brings it to your door.

How it works

Browse local stores, add what you need to your cart, pay securely online, and track your order as it makes its way to you. Simple as that.

For local businesses

We help local stores reach more customers without the cost and complexity of building their own delivery system. If that's you, we'd love to have you on board — get in touch using the details on our support page.
